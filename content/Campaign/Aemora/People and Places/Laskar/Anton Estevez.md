**Anton Estevez** is the elven butler for the Nenzune family. When [[Salvador Hernandez]] and [[Maria Nenzune Hernandez]] were both occupied with their professions, he would often fill in the gaps for childcare. As a result, he has a unique relationship with [[Friti Hernandez]].

His history with the Nenzune family goes back centuries, where his loyalty has earned him a comfortable and consistent role.
