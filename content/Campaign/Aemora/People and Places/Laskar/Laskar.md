**Laskar** is a city in the south-east of [[Aemora]] in the region formerly managed by the [[Silver Blade]]. It hosts the [[Mage's College]] and is the hometown of [[Betalia Demacras]] and her family.

The city is run by [[Syndra Kelcyne]], a middle-aged elf noble with ties to the College.
