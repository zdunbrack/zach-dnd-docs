**Aris Demacras** is the mother of [[Betalia Demacras]] and the wife of [[Leo Demacras]]. She works at the [[Mage's College]] in [[Laskar]] under the Augmentations department.

She specializes in nature spirits and fae creatures, having dived deep into exploring symbiotic relationships after Talia lost her arm to an ooze as a child.
