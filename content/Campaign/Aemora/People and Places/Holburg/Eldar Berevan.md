**Eldar Berevan** is an elven druid who lives on the outskirts of [[Holburg]]. He was kidnapped by sea devils and saved by [[The Party]] as their first contract. He is also [[Fibblestib Waggletop]]'s uncle by marriage.

Eldar follows [[Nilhitu]]'s teachings, trying to find balance and harmony in his relationship with nature. He acts as Holburg's local healer and elder, helping alleviate all sorts of ailments and troubles.
